What We Do

We connect people in the fashion industry with the latest trends, style insights, and insider gossip using our innovative AI algorithm. Whether you’re just starting out or you’re a seasoned expert, we bridge you directly to sellers and fashion professionals.

History

JeongYoon Choi was born in Seoul, South Korea. Driven by and having been exposed to constant challenge from peers and at home, she learned not only to adapt, but also to thrive academically and stay ahead of her competition. She graduated from KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science & Technology) with a Master’s in Engineering. Afterwards she worked as a consultant at Accenture and a strategic manager at Kakao Corp, overseeing several major acquisitions.

Purpose

Through her experience in technology and exposure to fashion in South Korea, our founder built myprojectRunway at the intersection of both worlds. Our mission is to empower users to connect with like-minded fashion enthusiasts and experts, discover emerging trends, and collaborate on style—making fashion more accessible, inclusive, and cutting-edge.
